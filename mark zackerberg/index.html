Tribute to Mark
Zuckerberg

Co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms

Mark Zuckerberg is an American technology entrepreneur best known for co-founding Facebook, now known as Meta. He launched the platform in 2004 while studying at Harvard University, aiming to connect people around the world. Today, he leads Meta in building social media and virtual reality technologies that shape how billions of people communicate and interact online.

Biography

  • Full Name: Mark Elliot Zuckerberg
  • Born: May 14, 1984, White Plain, New York
  • Attended Harvard University (Dropped out)
  • Co-founded Facebook in 2004
  • Married to Priscilla Chan, with two children
  • Net Worth: Frequently listed among the world's richest individuals.
  • Philanthropy: Co-founder of the Chan Zurkerberg Initiative, focusing on education, health, and social issues
  • Controversies: Faced criticism over privacy issues, data breaches, and the spread of misinformation on Facebook.
